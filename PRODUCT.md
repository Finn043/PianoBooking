# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Primary: Students**
- Book lessons across all devices, primarily mobile
- Quick booking decisions - pick weekly slot and go
- Context: Existing students booking weekly lessons, new students joining

**Secondary: Hannah (Admin)**
- Manages availability, students, and bookings
- Uses both mobile and laptop, primarily laptop
- Needs clear overview and efficient management

## Product Purpose

A piano class booking system that makes lesson scheduling effortless. Students quickly view and book available teaching slots without creating accounts. Hannah easily manages her calendar, students, and session tracking with automated notifications and Google Calendar sync.

Success: Student completes booking in under 2 minutes from landing to confirmation.

## Brand Personality

Professional, Friendly, Welcoming

**Voice:** Warm and encouraging, musically-inspired, confident guidance
**Tone:** Approachable expertise, not intimidating
**Emotional Goal:** Students feel welcomed and confident; Hannah feels organized and in control

## Anti-references

- **SaaS-cream** - Generic white/blue SaaS look
- **Too corporate/formal** - Stiff, enterprise software feel
- **Cold/clinical** - Lacks musical warmth and personal connection

## Design Principles

1. **Musical Warmth** - Piano-inspired visual elements without being cliché. Musical notes, keyboard patterns integrated tastefully.
2. **Mobile-First Speed** - Booking flow optimized for quick decisions on small screens. Don't make students scroll or tap more than necessary.
3. **Clarity Over Complexity** - Hannah sees what she needs at a glance; students see availability immediately. No hidden menus or mystery navigation.
4. **Reassuring Simplicity** - No-account booking feels trustworthy, not risky. Clear confirmation at every step.
5. **Playful Professionalism** - Fun enough to feel welcoming, serious enough to build confidence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Consider reduced motion for animations.
